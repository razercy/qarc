About QARC

We are the Quantum Advancement and Research Club (QARC) at IIT (ISM) Dhanbad, a student-led initiative established in association with NVCTI. We explore the frontiers of quantum computing and physics to foster innovation, share ideas, and build a thriving quantum community right here on campus.

Mission

  1. Master the Fundamentals: We’re building a rock-solid foundation in quantum technologies from the ground up.
  2. Theory → Reality: We are moving past the textbooks to explore real-world applications.
  3. Hardware Frontiers: Our journey starts with quantum computing and expands into hands-on, hardware-oriented projects.
